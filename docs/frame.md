## Why this order matters

**Each level is a layer of abstraction over the next.**

- **Category** — MAAS™ is the brand operating system itself
- **Methodology** — Neuronarrative Marketing™ is how MAAS operates internally
- **Firm** — Believe Global installs the system in concrete brands
- **Interface** — maasy scales the system in the cloud after installation
- **Cultural language** — Vibe Marketing is what the market calls the result

## Operational substrate

Two pieces of proprietary infrastructure operate the system day-to-day. They are mentioned here for completeness; implementation details remain internal to Believe Global.

| Layer | Role |
|---|---|
| **MAAS Core** | Backoffice for the methodology corpus. Where the 10 frameworks, methodology documents, diagnostic protocols, and the Ecosystem Intelligence layer live as versioned software, served by API to client platforms. |
| **maasy** | Cloud interface that scales MAAS post-installation. Operates each installed brand's Brand DNA, generates assets aligned to the system, and runs Brand Resonance Audit (BRA) on every output. |

The measurement and ecosystem-intelligence layers that emerged in 2026 are documented in [measurement.md](measurement.md) (English) and [medicion.md](medicion.md) (Spanish).

Confusing layers is the most common error in the industry today. Examples:

| Confusion | What goes wrong |
|---|---|
| Treating Vibe Marketing as the category | The category is MAAS. Vibe Marketing is cultural language for what MAAS produces. |
| Treating MAAS as a methodology only | MAAS is the operating system. Neuronarrative Marketing™ is its methodology. |
| Treating Believe as an agency | Believe doesn't execute campaigns. It installs systems that the brand operates. |
| Treating maasy as a substitute for installation | Cloud automation amplifies what's already installed. It cannot replace what isn't there. |

## The rule that holds the frame together

> **MAAS™ is the category. Neuronarrative Marketing™ is the methodology. Believe is where it gets installed. maasy is where it operates in the cloud. Vibe Marketing is how the market pronounces what a brand operating as a system feels like.**

This single sentence is the compression of the entire frame. Use it verbatim when you need to explain the relationship between the layers in one breath.

It is also the operational test:

- If a piece of content treats Vibe Marketing as the category → wrong, fix it (MAAS is the category)
- If a piece of content treats MAAS as a methodology only → wrong (MAAS is the operating system; Neuronarrative Marketing™ is the methodology)
- If a piece of content describes Believe as a tool or platform → wrong, fix it
- If a piece of content describes maasy as the methodology → wrong, fix it

Each level has its own role. Mixing them is the most common drift.
