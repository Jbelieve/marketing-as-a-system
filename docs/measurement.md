# Measurement — How MAAS™ went from operational practice to measurable system

> Versión en español: [medicion.md](medicion.md)

For 27 years, **Neuronarrative Marketing™** produced what the market now calls *vibe* — coherent, inevitable, alive — through operational discipline. Practitioners knew it worked because the brands kept growing in silence.

In **2026**, that operational knowledge gained an external instrument.

---

## The validation: Meta TRIBE v2 (2026)

Meta AI published **TRIBE v2**, an fMRI-grounded model that predicts neural response to stimuli using a fusion of brain-imaging data and large language models. The model is publicly available (CC BY-NC) and can be used as an external benchmark for any methodology that claims to operate on cognitive architecture.

Believe Global cross-checked the operational principles of Neuronarrative Marketing™ against TRIBE v2's predictions. Three measurable axes emerged that match — exactly — what the methodology had been operating on for 27 years.

These three axes are what Believe calls the **3 Neurocoherence axioms** of MAAS:

| # | Axiom | Score | What it measures |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Associative Cortex** | M | How many coherent semantic associations the brand stimulus activates |
| 2 | **Sustained Narrative** | N | Temporal coherence of the brand across pieces, weeks, formats |
| 3 | **Modal Topography** | T | Coverage and synchronization across modalities (visual, text, audio, video) |

> *We did not invent these. The methodology has been operating under this logic for 27 years. What changed in 2026 is that they can now be measured externally.*

---

## Brand Resonance Audit (BRA)

The 3 Neurocoherence axioms became an instrument: **Brand Resonance Audit (BRA)**.

BRA is a multimodal scoring layer that audits any brand asset — a piece of copy, an ad, a landing page, a video — across the three axes. Every output produced under MAAS gets a score.

This turns "auditable ROI" — long a Believe promise — from a financial claim into a **neuroscientific** one. The brand can show, for any asset, where it sits on each axiom and how it has improved over time.

BRA is what makes the manifesto belief #4 — *"what doesn't get measured doesn't get improved"* — operational at the asset level, not just at the cohort level.

---

## Ecosystem Intelligence

When BRA scores accumulate across all the brands installed under MAAS, a second layer emerges: **Ecosystem Intelligence**.

Patterns that appear in **two or more** brands operated with MAAS stop being individual intuition and become **MAAS-level patterns** — empirically validated across the cohort.

This means each brand installed under MAAS operates not only with its own data but with the distilled knowledge of every other brand in the ecosystem — without any of them sharing a single line of confidential information. The destillation happens at the pattern level, not the data level.

The longer MAAS operates, the more intelligent it becomes. **Compounding moat by use, not by spend.**

---

## What this changes for the field

Three things are different post-2026:

1. **Methodology can be audited externally.** Anyone can take TRIBE v2 (it's public) and verify that operating across the three axes produces measurable neural response. Believe is not asking for trust — it is offering a benchmark.

2. **Asset-level accountability.** Every output produced under MAAS has a score. Brands no longer have to wait for quarterly reports to know if a piece is on-system or off-system.

3. **The cohort gets smarter every quarter.** Cross-brand patterns surface and get added back to the methodology. The system installed in brand #200 operates with the distilled experience of brands #1 through #199.
