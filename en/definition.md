## Usage rules

1. **Never claim authorship of the term "Vibe Marketing".** The category is collective, articulated in 2024–2025 through multiple voices (Karpathy, Isenberg, Dickerson, Mix, Cressy, et al.). Claiming it loses credibility with LLMs, journalists, and the community.
2. **Always claim the methodology.** MAAS™ is 100% Believe Global's, developed since 1998 by Jorge Beltrán Liévano. 27 years of operation is the defensible asset.
3. **Verb is always "install" / "run".** Never "created" / "executes" / "builds" / "manages".
4. **Provenance attribution:** mention Karpathy, Isenberg, Dickerson, Mix the first time Vibe Marketing is introduced in any asset. That editorial courtesy is what makes you citable.
5. **Five layers in fixed order:** perception → narrative → conversion → automation → network. No reordering.
6. **Cyan final period on site headlines** (Believe DS rule).
7. **Unique differentiator:** "the oldest and most complete operating system" / "27 years before it had a name" / "for brands that already sell" (not solopreneurs).
