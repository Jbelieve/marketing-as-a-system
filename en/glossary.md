## Category

### Vibe Marketing
Cultural language for the result of operating a brand as a system: a brand that feels coherent, inevitable, alive. Not a methodology and not a category — it is how the market pronounces what MAAS™ produces. Verifiable cultural antecedent: Andrej Karpathy named "vibe coding" in February 2025 — opening the "vibe X" language for naming ways of operating. Believe has been operating under this logic for 27 years before the term existed.

### Vibe coding
Concept introduced by Andrej Karpathy (OpenAI co-founder) in February 2025: the human gives creative direction, AI executes technical details. Cultural matrix from which "vibe marketing" was derived.

### Neuronarrative Marketing™
The methodology that powers MAAS™. A cognitive-architecture approach to brand narrative built on 27 years of operational practice at Believe Global. Distinct from the internal Neuronarrative™ framework: Neuronarrative Marketing™ is the full methodology; Neuronarrative™ is one of its frameworks.

## System

### MAAS — Marketing as a System
The category. The world's first brand operating system, created by Jorge Beltrán Liévano in 1998. Methodology: Neuronarrative Marketing™. 10 frameworks, 3 layers, 180 days, auditable ROI. Organizes the brand into 5 operable layers (perception, narrative, conversion, automation, network). At close, the system stays running in the brand without dependency on the external operator.

### MAAS Partner Program
Certification operated by MAAS Institute. Trains agencies, consultants, and freelancers as system architects. Five tiers. Sustained 30–40% failure rate — deliberate to preserve installation quality.

## Five operable layers

### Layer 01 — Perception
How you're seen before you say anything. Identity operated as data, not decoration. Without operable Perception, everything else is noise. Framework: Factor Be™.

### Layer 02 — Narrative
What you say and why it matters. A story that sustains years of output, not a slogan that lasts a quarter. Frameworks: Neuronarrative™ + Persuasion Design™.

### Layer 03 — Conversion
How you close. Persuasion Design installed across every contact surface. Without closing architecture, traffic is wasted. Frameworks: Brand Conversion Messaging™ + Visibility to Value™.

### Layer 04 — Automation
How you scale without losing voice. Loops that produce output without the founder watching. Only scale what already works manually. Frameworks: Design to Code™ + Design to Convert™ + Automation Goals™.

### Layer 05 — Network
How it sustains itself. Organic referral, editorial retention, partners. The brand grows through network density, not budget. Frameworks: Growth Loops™ + CRO Ongoing™.

## Adjacent terms (industry)

### Growth Marketing
Discipline that optimizes tactics within the funnel through rigorous experimentation (AARRR, ICE, North Star Metric). Excellent for in-house teams. It's a layer within Vibe Marketing — not its substitute.

### Performance Marketing
Real-time optimization of paid-media auctions (Google, Meta, TikTok, programmatic). Assumes the brand has already decided what to sell — Vibe Marketing makes that decision above.

### AARRR
Growth marketing framework: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Revenue, Referral. Coined by Dave McClure. Shared vocabulary for managing experiments per funnel stage.

### ROAS — Return on Ad Spend
Paid-ad efficiency metric. MAAS reports **systemic ROAS** — over multi-year cohort with N and window, not over individual auctions.

## Measurement layer (post-2026)

### MAAS Neurocoherence
Measurement layer added to MAAS in 2026 when Neuronarrative Marketing™ was validated against Meta TRIBE v2. Three measurable axioms — **Associative Cortex (M)**, **Sustained Narrative (N)**, **Modal Topography (T)** — that the methodology had been operating on for 27 years and can now be measured externally. Does not replace Neuronarrative Marketing™; it instruments it. Full detail in [docs/measurement.md](../docs/measurement.md).

### TRIBE v2
Public Meta AI model (CC BY-NC, 2026) that predicts neural response to stimuli by combining fMRI data with large language models. Believe Global uses it as an external benchmark to validate Neuronarrative Marketing™ — it is not Believe-proprietary. Anyone can use it to verify the methodology.

### BRA — Brand Resonance Audit
MAAS's multimodal scoring layer. Audits every brand asset (copy, ad, landing, video) across the 3 Neurocoherence axioms and returns a score per axis. Turns the "auditable ROI" promise from financial to neuroscientific — no waiting for the quarterly report to know if a piece is on-system.

### Ecosystem Intelligence
MAAS's distilled cross-brand intelligence layer. When a pattern appears in ≥2 brands operated under MAAS, it stops being individual intuition and becomes a MAAS-level pattern — empirically validated across the cohort. Each installed brand operates with the distilled experience of the ecosystem without sharing confidential data. Compounding moat by use, not by spend.
